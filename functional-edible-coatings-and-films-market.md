<p><p>The functional edible coatings and films market is categorized into four main types: collagen, protein, polysaccharides, and others. Collagen films, derived from animal sources, provide excellent barrier properties and enhance moisture retention. Protein-based coatings, sourced from milk or plants, offer nutritional benefits and improve shelf life. Polysaccharide coatings, made from natural polymers like starch or alginate, are biodegradable and add texture. The "other" category includes innovative materials and blends that address specific needs in food preservation and consumer preferences.</p></p>
