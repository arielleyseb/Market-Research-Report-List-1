<p><p>Oocyte retrieval needles are essential tools in assisted reproductive technology for collecting eggs from the ovaries. The market features two primary types: single lumen and double lumen needles. Single lumen needles, designed with a single channel, facilitate straightforward egg aspiration. In contrast, double lumen needles have two channels, allowing simultaneous aspiration and flushing, enhancing efficiency and reducing procedure time. Both types cater to different physician preferences and patient needs, influencing their selection in various fertility clinics.</p></p>
